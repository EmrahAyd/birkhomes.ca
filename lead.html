How Much Does A Bathroom Renovation Project Really Cost?

When planning your bathroom renovation, one of the primary factors to consider is labor cost. Contractors typically charge an hourly rate depending on their experience and qualifications, ranging from $40+ per hour to more substantial fees for more experienced workers. The selection of a contractor providing comprehensive services is recommended as it facilitates access to various tradespeople, such as designers, plumbers, electricians, etc., all under one roof. This can be used to advantage as it simplifies the financial planning process, as well as allowing for the possibility of substantial discounts. The expense for restroom renovation typically ranges from $6000-$40,000, which accounts for both the labor and materials necessary for the remodelling process.
